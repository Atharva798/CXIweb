UPConnect and Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG)

SuperCharge Your Market Strategy:

Discover how integrating UPC data transforms your understanding of consumer behavior, allowing you to reach and engage a broader audience based on their food and beverage purchase behavior.

UPConnect

  • Connect the Dots: Match UPC codes from products to similar ingredients in restaurant menu items.
  • Expand Your Reach: Visualize the total audiences that interact with these menu items, going beyond traditional retail customers.
  • Key Benefit: Provides a unique advantage by revealing potential new customer bases not visible through standard retail analytics.

Media Activation

Engage and Convert with Precision

  • Targeted Media: Commingle CustomerX.i audiences based
    on PBT to send media and promotional materials to selected audiences, driving AFH (Away From Home) and retail sales.
  • Measurement: Off-platform attribution to measure incrementality and accurately optimize media spend.

CrossDine Insights

Leverage Loyalty for Deeper Insights

  • Loyalty Insights: Analyze purchase data from loyalty program members to see their food and beverage choices outside of retail.
  • Cross-Retail Behavior: Gain insights into cross-retail behavior to tailor offerings and promotions in line with actual customer habits.
