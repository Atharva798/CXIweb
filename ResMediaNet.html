Restaurant Media Network™

Clients use CustomerX.i’s Restaurant Media Network™ to reach the right guest at any time and at the right time to grow their stores.

Empowering Restaurants with Advanced Data Analytics & Personalized Marketing

Integrated Marketing Ecosystem:

  • Extensive Reach: Use CX.i’s RMN to tap into a database of over 150 million customers, tracking and analyzing data from billions in weekly transactions.
  • Deep Customer Insights: Access detailed demographic information, purchase behaviors, and interaction data, allowing for precise customer segmentation and targeted advertising.


Operational Efficiency & Customer Engagement:

  • Ad Activation & Platform Optimization: Design personalized ads tailored to the behaviors and preferences of identified guest segments. Deploy these ads across optimal platforms like CTV, Google, and YouTube to maximize engagement.

Enhanced Menu & Market Strategies:

  • Menu-Level Performance: Understand how menu items relate to demographics and revenue, track performance by price and sales, and compare your pricing with competitors.
  • Market Analysis: Identify potential new audiences, understand demographics, and optimize engagement to measure attribution tied to sales lift and incrementality.
