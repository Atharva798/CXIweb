Querencia: translated means "Favorite Place". And with our AI engine that is exactly what we harness the power to create for each of our restaurant brands at every single one of their locations. Creating a favorite place for their guest.

Align marketing, operations, and front-line
We deliver services that support small emerging to large enterprise restaurants

TOTAL MARKET INSIGHTS (TMI™)

CustomerX.i helps stores see how they rank locally and compare to their competitors in the market.
With our innovative technology, we are able to narrow down your competitors by cuisine.
Your store will be able to compare factors such as sales, foot traffic, demographics, and more!

Learn more

CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHIC INSIGHTS (CDI™)

This solution is designed to graph your store's syndicated audiences. We use this information to understand and analyze how far existing customers are willing to travel to visit your store. We also use this information to help find potential customers in your area.

Learn more

MENU-LEVEL INSIGHTS (MLI™ )

MLI™ dashboards are configurable allowing restaurant operators and marketing teams to compare store performance and guest needs, as well as being able to break down menu items and their direct impact on guests. Some features include:

  • Menu level buys tied to guests' demographics.
  • Day part breakdown of menu item performance. (MoM/YoY)
  • LTOs compared to guests' demographics vs other menu items.
  • Menu items tied to pricing, location, and more!
Learn more
