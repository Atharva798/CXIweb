Customer Transactional Insights

CTI is a dashboard used to visualize many factors of transaction data such as what time guests buy, what means they use to pay, and what channel they use to buy from your store.

Features

Day Part

Understand what time customers buy at your store(s), days of the week, and how they buy.

Tender

Understand how customers pay: Apple Pay, Credit Card, App, Cash, etc.

Channel

Understand how they buy, via in-store, online or drive-thru.



Customer Segmentation

Analyze what categories customers fall into such as New customers, Existing customers, Loyal Customers, and At-Risk Customers.
